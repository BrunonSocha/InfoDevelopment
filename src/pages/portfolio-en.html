KSeF Integration - Modern Solutions for Businesses

In the era of digitization and the obligation to issue structured invoices through the National e-Invoice System (KSeF), our company offers comprehensive integration solutions that allow entrepreneurs to fully leverage the potential of automating financial processes.

We design, implement, and maintain systems integrating any client IT environment with the KSeF platform.

We offer:

  • Automatic issuing and sending of invoices to KSeF in XML format compliant with the FA(2) schema,
  • Receiving and validating UPO (Official Receipt Confirmation),
  • Handling incoming invoices - automatic downloading, verification, and import of data into accounting systems,
  • Integration with databases (MySQL, MSSQL, PostgreSQL) and popular accounting systems,
  • Administrative panel and dashboard with a preview of invoice statuses and integration errors,
  • Integration of KSeF REST API with existing client applications.

Automation of Price Reporting to dane.gov

A complete system for automatic price reporting and data publication to dane.gov.pl - from extraction and validation, through scheduling and versioning, to monitoring and audit.

What we deliver:

  • Data connectors: import from databases (SQL/NoSQL), files (CSV/XLSX/JSON), API, and SFTP; mapping fields to dane.gov schemas.
  • Validation and data quality: business and legal rules, anomaly detection, preview of changes before publication.
  • Schedules and retries: cyclical submissions (CRON), intelligent retries, prioritized queuing.
  • Dataset versioning: history of changes, comparisons, 'roll-back' capability.
  • Audit trail: who/what/when - full operation logs and action signatures.

Two deployment models (to choose from):

1. Managed service in our environment (SaaS)

  • 2-center redundancy (active-active/active-standby) - automatic failover.
  • 24/7 Monitoring & alerting (availability, delays, validation errors, API limits), dashboards, and notifications.
  • Archiving and retention: storage policies, snapshots, point-in-time recovery.
  • Data CMS: panel for managing datasets, metadata, schemas, permissions, and publication.
  • SLA and support: clearly defined response times, care from the engineering team.

2. Autonomous system at the client's premises (on-prem / private cloud)

  • Delivered as containers/VM with full documentation and installation scripts.
  • HA options (cluster/duplication), backups, and recovery policies.
  • 'Zero-downtime' updates, schema migrations, and regression tests in a standardized pipeline.

Add-ons and extensions:

  • Business rules per dataset (e.g., rounding, price thresholds, aggregations).
  • Integrations with ERP/CRM/data warehouse; webhooks and API for orchestration.
  • Management reports: publication status, errors, trends, and resource completeness.
