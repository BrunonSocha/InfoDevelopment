Is your company ready for September 11, 2025?

The new act on the transparency of apartment prices introduces an obligation that cannot be ignored.

New reporting obligation

From September 11, 2025, every developer will be required to regularly submit data on the prices of sold apartments to the government portal. Read the act.

Risk of high penalties

Non-compliance with the new regulations is subject to a financial penalty imposed by UOKiK, reaching up to 10% of annual turnover.
